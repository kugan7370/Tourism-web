Ayutthaya Historical Park

Ayutthaya Historical Park covers the ruins of the old city of Ayutthaya, Phra Nakhon Si Ayutthaya Province, Thailand.

Khao Yai National Park

Khao Yai National Park covers more than 2,000 square kilometers of forest and grassland in central Thailand.

Sukhothai Historical Park

Sukhothai Historical Park covers the ruins of Sukhothai, literally 'dawn of happiness', Thailand.

\

Thung Yai Wildlife Reserve

Thung Yai Naresuan Wildlife Sanctuary is a UNESCO-protected world heritage site, along with the neighbouring Huai Kha Khaeng Park.

Ban Chiang

Ban Chiang Archaeological Site is a prehistoric mound in Udon Thani of North-Eastern Thailand.

Wat Phra Mahathat

Located in Southern Thailand, the Wat Phra Mahathat is a Buddhist temple housing the tooth of the Buddha as a chedi or a stupa.

Phu Phra Bat Historical Park

Similar to the Ban Chiang, the Phu Phra Bat in Udon Thani Province is a site of prehistoric ruins.

Chiang Mai Province

The cityscape of Chiang Mai in Northern Thailand is nominated as a UNESCO world heritage site for its 300 Buddhist temples (wat).

Kaeng Krachan National Park

The largest national park in Thailand, Kaeng Krachan National Park lies in the vicinity of Hua Hin, a popular beach resort town in Myanmar.
